Pokédex-over-DNS

Sainternet is proud to present the world's first (…and only?) DNS driven TXT record based National Pokédex!

A somewhat fun (ab)use of DNS enabling one to query a Pokémon species (currently supported: English) in order to receive its National Pokédex Number (NPN), or query an NPN (zero padding optional) in order to receive the Pokémon species name (currently supported: English, Japanese Rōmaji).
