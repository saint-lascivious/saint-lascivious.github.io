iOS/macOS Made Easy

Friday, 18 November 2022 22:30+13:00 (NZDT)

Previously relegated to the realm of "if you know, you know", I am very much pleased to announce that that is no longer the case and it is much easier for anyone with an Apple iPhone or Mac to connect to Sainternet DNS over HTTPS or DNS over TLS (iOS 14/macOS Big Sur or higher required).

You can choose your preferred DNS Profile (DoH, DoT, DoH/DoT) and get started here.
